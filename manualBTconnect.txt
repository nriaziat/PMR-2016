Click on Bluetooth icon in top right corner (next to the battery symbol)
Go to 'Bluetooth Settings'
There will be a plus sign in the lower left to add devices
Wait for the search to return user friendly names
Wait a little, then click on device once
Click on PIN-settings
Select PIN 1234
Connect to device
Confirm pin code on NXT screen when prompted

NOTE: If you do not see a bluetooth icon, then you either do not have
bluetooth hardware OR you connected the stick to a USB 3.0 port.
